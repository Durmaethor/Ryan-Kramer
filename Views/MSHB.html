My Simple Home Business

This was the first "full" website I built, and launched live, since I began pursuing front end development professionally. It is a website for a side business that my wife and I are working. So far it has brought some wonderful results for us, and I continue to brainstorm updates for it as I learn more. You can view the full website here.

My process for designing this site was this: I wanted a site that portrayed a home business, but that was also friendly and simple.I chose the fonts on Google Fonts that had the look and feel that I was after, and that also gave the feeling of a "home business". This kept the site lite and airy, and made us very approachable by people viewing it. The color scheme is also simple and warm, and meets our personal desire as well.
